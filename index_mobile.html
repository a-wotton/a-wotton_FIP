About the Pets

About Container

  • Dollyis a French Bulldog who joined the household during the summer of 2022, making her the newest addition to the family. The colour of her fur is fawn; light brown with a darker face. Dolly's mother is Daisy, who is our other French Bulldog. Dolly was part of Daisy's second litter of puppies.
  • Daisyis our other French Bulldog, and she has a blue-grey fur. She is around 4 years older than Dolly, and has had two litters of puppies, one in 2021, and another in 2022.
  • Percyis our Great Dane, his fur is harlequin, which is white with black and grey spots, similar to a Dalmatian. He has been in the family for the longest, ever since 2014.
  • Maggieis our Calico cat, with white fur and spots of brown, grey and black. Maggie became a member of the family back in 2015.
