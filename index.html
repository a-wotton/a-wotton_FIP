About the Pets

  • Dolly is a French Bulldog who joined the household during the summer of 2022, making her the newest addition to the family. The colour of her fur is fawn; light brown with a darker face.   • Daisy is our other French Bulldog, and she has a blue-grey fur. She is several years older than Dolly, and has had two litters of puppies, one in 2021, and another in 2022.
  • Percy Percy is our Great Dane, his fur is harlequin, which is white with black and grey spots, similar to a Dalmatian. He has been in the family for the longest, ever since 20XX.
  • Maggie Maggie is our Calico cat, with white fur and spots of brown, grey and black.
